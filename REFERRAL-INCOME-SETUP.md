# Binance Referral Income Setup

## Main Account → Business Account Referral Structure

### Step 1: Get Your Referral Link
1. Login to your MAIN personal account
2. Go to: https://www.binance.com/en/activity/referral/offers
3. Copy your referral ID/link

### Step 2: Create Business Account with Referral
Use your referral link format:
```
https://accounts.binance.com/register?ref=YOUR_REFERRAL_ID
```

### Important: Referral Rules
- ✅ You CAN refer business accounts you control
- ✅ Business account trades count for commission
- ✅ P2P trading generates referral income
- ✅ Spot trading also generates commission

### Referral Commission Structure:

#### Standard Rates:
- **Spot Trading**: 20% of trading fees
- **Futures Trading**: 10% of trading fees  
- **P2P Trading**: No direct commission (but drives spot volume)

#### With BNB Payment (25% extra):
- **Spot Trading**: 25% of fees (20% + 5% bonus)
- **Futures Trading**: 12.5% of fees

### Maximizing Referral Income:

1. **High-Volume P2P → Spot Conversion**
   - P2P trades need spot conversion
   - Each USDT/INR trade = spot fee
   - 200k USDT monthly = significant fees

2. **Referral Kickback Settings**
   - Give 10% kickback to business account
   - You keep 10-15% commission
   - Business account saves on fees

3. **Volume Milestones**:
   - 50 BTC: 20% → 20% rate
   - 500 BTC: 20% → 30% rate
   - 1000 BTC: 20% → 40% rate

### Setup Process:

1. **Get Referral Link** (from main account):
   ```
   Dashboard → Referral → Copy Default Link
   ```

2. **Register Business Account**:
   - Open incognito/new browser
   - Use your referral link
   - Select "Entity Account"
   - Complete registration

3. **Verify Connection**:
   - Check main account → Referral Dashboard
   - Should show "1 Pending Referral"
   - Becomes active after KYC
